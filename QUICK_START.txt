Q: Frontend shows "Failed to fetch"
A: Set NEXT_PUBLIC_API_URL in Vercel and redeploy.

Q: Docker build fails on HF Spaces
A: Check requirements.txt pinned versions match your local environment.

Q: Neo4j connection timeout
A: Verify NEO4J_URI (should be neo4j+s://f4997b01.databases.neo4j.io)
